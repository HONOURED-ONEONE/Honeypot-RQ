# Intent-tagged examples for vLLM rephrase
# Format:
#   [INTENT=...]
#   <one statement (no "?") + one question (ends with "?")>
# Notes:
# - Do NOT say "red flag" or "red flags".
# - The statement should refer to what the scammer asked for or claimed (OTP/PIN/link/threat/urgency).
# - The question must target ONE artifact category only.

[INTENT=INT_ASK_OFFICIAL_HELPLINE]
I’m not comfortable sharing an OTP on chat. What is the official helpline number I should call to verify this?

[INTENT=INT_ASK_OFFICIAL_HELPLINE]
You’re pressing me to act immediately and share sensitive details. Which official support number should I call to confirm this request?

[INTENT=INT_ASK_OFFICIAL_WEBSITE]
I’m wary of opening links sent under time pressure. What is the official website or domain I should check instead?

[INTENT=INT_ASK_OFFICIAL_WEBSITE]
You’re asking me to use a portal link during an urgent warning. Which official site should I open to verify this independently?

[INTENT=INT_ASK_TICKET_REF]
This “blocked in minutes” warning is making me hesitant. What is the reference or complaint ID for this case?

[INTENT=INT_ASK_TICKET_REF]
You asked for OTP along with my account details. What is the case or ticket ID associated with this request?

[INTENT=INT_ASK_DEPARTMENT_BRANCH]
You claimed to be from the fraud team and asked for OTP. Which department or branch is handling this case?

[INTENT=INT_ASK_DEPARTMENT_BRANCH]
You’re presenting this as an official fraud alert and pushing for immediate action. Which office or team is responsible for this verification?

[INTENT=INT_ASK_ALT_VERIFICATION]
I won’t share OTP or PIN through chat. What alternative verification method is available through official channels?

[INTENT=INT_ASK_ALT_VERIFICATION]
You’re urging me to act fast and share sensitive information. What different verification option can I use instead?

[INTENT=INT_REFUSE_SENSITIVE_ONCE]
You asked for OTP or PIN, and I won’t share that on chat. Which official channel should I use to verify this safely?

[INTENT=INT_CHANNEL_FAIL]
You sent a link and asked me to verify right away. Which official website should I try instead?

[INTENT=INT_SECONDARY_FAIL]
You’re insisting I complete this immediately through chat. What other official option can I use to verify it?

[INTENT=INT_CLOSE_AND_VERIFY_SELF]
You asked for OTP/PIN and pushed urgency, so I will verify independently through official channels. Thank you.
